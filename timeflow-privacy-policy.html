Privacy Policy for TimeFlow Last updated: April 25, 2026 TimeFlow is a time management and routine timer app developed by Frosty Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data. 1. Data Collection TimeFlow does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with third parties. 2. Local Data Storage TimeFlow may store routine names, timer blocks, timer durations, completion history, selected language preferences, and app settings locally on the user's device. This information is stored only to make the app function properly and to preserve the user's routines between app sessions. 3. Audio Alerts TimeFlow may play local audio alerts during timer sessions. These audio files are included inside the app and do not require sending any data outside the device. 4. Internet Access TimeFlow does not require an internet connection for its main timer and routine features. 5. Third-Party Services TimeFlow does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or external user profiling services. 6. Children's Privacy TimeFlow is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal data, no children's personal information is transmitted or shared. 7. Data Security Since TimeFlow stores routine data locally on the device, users are responsible for managing access to their own device. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored data. 8. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. 9.
